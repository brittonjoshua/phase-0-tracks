I Dig a pony

It's true. For the sake of rock and roll, I've found it best to dig ponies as opposed to zebras or horses. It's most important to express to the pony your intentions to dig him/her. By "digging" a pony, we of course mean to appreciate at a respectful level.

Here's a few ways to dig ponies...

  • brush their manes
  • feed them carrots
  • play them classical music

Dug ponies will, in return...

  1. love you
  2. protect your farm
  3. plow your fields
